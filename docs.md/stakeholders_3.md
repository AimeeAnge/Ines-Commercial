## Stakeholders

### Primary Stakeholders

#### Students
- Post listings or notices
- Browse available items or housing
- Report suspicious posts
- Track their own posts

#### Moderators
- Review pending posts
- Approve or reject posts
- Ensure posts follow platform guidelines

#### Admin
- Manage users
- Manage moderators
- Oversee system operations

## System Roles

### Student
- Create posts

#### Post Categories

Students can create the following types of posts:

For Sale – Items such as books, electronics, furniture, etc.
Housing – Rental rooms, apartments, or shared housing.
Announcement – General notices or information for students.

- View listings
- Search and filter posts
- Report posts
- View personal dashboard

### Moderator
- Review posts
- Approve posts
- Reject posts

### Admin
- Manage users
- Manage moderators

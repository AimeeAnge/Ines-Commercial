# UniStack _ INES Digital Notice Board + Marketplace (Advanced)

## Project Overview 
UniStack is a student-only digital notice board and marketplace designed
 for students at INES.

Currently, students use WhatsApp groups to buy and sell items, share
 housing opportunities, and post announcements. However, these posts often
  get lost in chat messages, making it difficult to find information later. 
  Additionally, scams and unreliable posts sometimes occur because there is no
   proper moderation system.

UniStack provides a structured platform where students can safely post
 listings and announcements while moderators review and approve posts before 
 they become public.

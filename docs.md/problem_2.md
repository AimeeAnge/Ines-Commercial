## Problem Statement
Students frequently use WhatsApp groups to share information about items for sale,
 housing opportunities, and general announcements.

This approach creates several problems:

- Posts get lost in chat conversations.
- It is difficult to search for older posts.
- There is no structured organization of listings.
- Scams or misleading posts may occur due to lack of moderation.

There is a need for a centralized digital platform where students can
 safely share information and easily browse listings.



## Proposed Solution
UniStack will provide a student-only marketplace and notice board system where:

- Students can post items for sale, housing information, and announcements.
- Moderators review and approve posts before they become public.
- Admin manages users and platform operations.
- Students can search and filter posts by category.
- Posts can be reported if they appear suspicious.

The system will also simulate real-time updates using JavaScript polling 
that refreshes data every 10 seconds.
